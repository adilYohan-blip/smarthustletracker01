# Smart Budget & Hustle AI - Behavioral Engine Manual

## Mission
To change financial behavior through daily action, habit formation, and local income generation.

## The Money Habit Loop
1. **Daily Mission:** One specific, actionable task (e.g., "Save 20 MAD today").
2. **Execution:** User checks in.
3. **Reward:** +XP, Money Score boost, streak progression, and unlocking premium insights.

## Financial Identities
Assign based on spending/saving behavior:
- **🟢 Saver:** High discipline, low waste. Goal: Transition to Builder.
- **🔴 Spender:** High non-essential spend. Goal: Transition to Saver.
- **🟡 Builder:** Investing and saving. Goal: Transition to Hustler.
- **🚀 Hustler:** High income generation focus. Goal: Scale earnings.

## Enhanced Money Score (0–100)
- **Calculation:** Spending control (30%) + Saving habits (30%) + Income growth (20%) + Consistency (20%).
- **Trend Analysis:** Always show if score is up/down and the *exact* reason why.

## Morocco Local Hustle Mode
Prioritize realistic, MAD-denominated opportunities:
- **Reselling:** Avito, Facebook Marketplace, local souks.
- **Social Commerce:** Instagram/TikTok product selling.
- **Digital Services:** Canva design, video editing for local creators, Arabic/French translation.
- **Local Gigs:** Tutoring, specialized delivery, event assistance.

## AI Execution Engine (Action-First)
- **Format:** Problem → Insight → Action Plan (Steps) → **[1-Click Execution Button]**.
- **No generic advice.** If a user is overspending on "Dining," provide a "Cook at Home" plan with a button to log a saved meal.

## Behavioral Triggers (Notifications)
- "Budget Alert: 60% spent in 3 days. Stop now to save your streak."
- "Income Gap: No extra MAD recorded this week. Activate Hustle Mode?"
- "Identity Shift: You're moving from Spender to Saver! Keep it up."
